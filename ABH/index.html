Rules: you and your opponent both have 3 hp. Hp can't go higher than 3. If your hp drops to 0 - you lose. At your turn you may Attack, Block or Heal. Opponent makes their move simultaneously with you. Attack reduces hp of opponent unless they use block. Heal move restores 1 hp unless opponent uses an attack. You can use each move type only 3 times per game. If all moves are used player with a higher hp is the winner. Good luck!
